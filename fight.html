About

Fight translates the language of classic arcade fighting games — Street Fighter, Mortal Kombat — into a tabletop brawl. Every input, every combo, every punishing hit lands the same way it did at the cabinet.

Each turn, two cards are flipped from a shared deck, giving players four options to choose from: two directions and two buttons. Pick one and place a token on the matching input of your combo card. Race to fill your combo before anyone else fills theirs — finish it and you get to attack.

Getting hit has teeth. Take damage and you lose any tokens that aren't part of a continuous chain — your half-built combo shatters, and you're back to grinding inputs while everyone else is one move closer to landing on you.
