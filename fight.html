About

Fight is a fast paced free for all brawl. Every turn players select from the available 4 cards, placing a block on a matching square on their move cards. Complete one of your moves by covering all its squares and you can attack another player. When players get hit they lose health and any non-continuous placed blocks.

Game Details

  • Status In Development
  • Players 2–6
  • Play Time 20 minutes
  • Mechanics Flip and Place Royal Rumble Drafting
